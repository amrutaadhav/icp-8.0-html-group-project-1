Fees:80,000/-(1st Yr Fees)

Duration:2 Years

Eligibility:Admission to the MCA course is done through the TANCET (Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test) entrance exam. This is the only postgraduate course at the Madras Christian College, Chennai, whose admission is based on a separate entrance exam that the students need to give. The interested students need to register for the MCA exam on the TANCET portal separately, and then upload their scores for selection into the MCA course at MCC. The other prerequisite is for the students to have graduated with their BCA degree from a recognized university with at least a 50% score. For students in the final year of their BCA graduation degree, their scores up to the fifth semester would be taken into account.
